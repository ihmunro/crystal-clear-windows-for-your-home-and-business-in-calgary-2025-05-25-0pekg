Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule professional window cleaning?

For optimal results, we recommend residential windows be cleaned every 3-4 months and commercial properties every 1-2 months. However, frequency can vary based on your location, environment, and specific needs.

What cleaning solutions do you use?

We use professional-grade, eco-friendly cleaning solutions that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment. Our products are specifically designed to leave no residue and provide a streak-free finish.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes, we are fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind. Our comprehensive coverage protects both our workers and your property during the cleaning process.

What areas do you service in Calgary?

We service all areas within Calgary and surrounding communities, including residential homes, commercial buildings, and high-rise properties throughout the metropolitan area.

Do you offer any guarantees?

Yes, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our service, we'll return to address any concerns at no additional cost within 7 days of service.
