Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our window cleaning services

How often should I have my windows professionally cleaned?

For optimal results and maintaining your property's appearance, we recommend professional window cleaning every 3-6 months. However, this can vary based on your location, weather conditions, and personal preferences. Commercial properties may benefit from more frequent cleaning to maintain a professional image.

Are your cleaning solutions safe and eco-friendly?

Absolutely! We're committed to environmental responsibility and use only biodegradable, eco-friendly cleaning products that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment. Our solutions are highly effective while being gentle on your windows and surrounding landscaping.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we offer completely free, no-obligation quotes for all our window cleaning services. We'll assess your specific needs, discuss your preferences, and provide a transparent estimate with no hidden fees. Contact us today to schedule your free consultation.

What makes Crystal Clear Windows different from other services?

Our commitment to excellence sets us apart. We combine years of experience, premium eco-friendly products, advanced cleaning techniques, and a 100% satisfaction guarantee. Our team is fully insured and bonded, and we pride ourselves on reliability, professionalism, and exceptional customer service.
